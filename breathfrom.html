Breath From

A container for capturing human breath. This balloon can be inflated and kept as a keepsake or given as a gift to commemorate a special moment. BREATH offers a unique way to preserve fleeting memories. Made entirely from thermoplastic polyurethane, it holds up to 500 millilitres of air, which is roughly the average volume exhaled in a single breath by an adult. Users can write directly on the balloon to note whose breath it contains and the date it was exhaled, preserving it for posterity. Crowdfunding attempt on Kickstarter was unsuccessful.
