Breath From

A container to keep human breath. The balloon can be inflated and kept as a memento or given as a gift to cherish a special memory. It could be a perfect way to remember a rare occasion, such as a visit with a grandparent, a proposal, or an unforgettable meal, even if it made your breath garlicky. With BREATH, all these moments can be easily remembered. It is made 100% of thermoplastic polyurethane and holds 500 milliliters of air. People can write directly on the balloon to note whose breath it contains, and the date they exhaled for posterity. Crowdfunding failed on Kickstarter
