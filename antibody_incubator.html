Antibody Incubator

A conceptual home healthcare device that incubates targeted antibodies. This device collects data from a biometric chip worn by the user and uses it to design a diet tailored to the user’s specific nutritional needs. Designed for everyday use, it can be mounted under the dining table. Exhibited at Gwangju Design Biennale 2017, Gwangju, Korea.
