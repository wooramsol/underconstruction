Antibody Incubator

A conceptual home healthcare device incubating targeted antibodies. This device collects information received from a biometric chip worn by the user, and uses that information to design a diet that perfectly satisfies the user’s specific nutritional needs. It is designed to be mounted under the dining table for everyday use.

exhibited at 2017 Gwangju Design Biennale, South Korea
