Butane Canister Recycling Tool

Butane gas canisters are among the most common types of gas containers, but recycling them is often inconvenient—and sometimes even dangerous. To recycle them safely, a hole must be punctured to release pressure and any remaining gas, making the metal safe for disposal. Most people need to carry a separate tool for this, but even that small inconvenience can discourage proper recycling. This concept proposes a simple solution: a built-in metal clip in the canister itself. Inexpensive to manufacture and easy to use, the clip punctures the can when struck against a flat surface.
