A2 Lamp

A laser-cut table lamp inspired by a classic design. Designed to be made locally using any A2 paper-sized material at a maker space. The 2D lamp drawing is freely available for download under a Creative Commons (CC) licence. Exhibited at Seoul Design Festival 2021, Seoul, Korea. Download CAD file: https://cults3d.com/:1063994
